Selected projects

  • College dropout

    The task is to predict student dropout, graduation, or extended enrollment using a multiclass classification approach. The data includes student demographics, academic path, and performance information.

    Regression task. Vector data concerning variables such as age, location, distance to amenities and purchase price was collected. The goal is to predict the price of real estate based on these variables.

    Regression task trying to predict concrete compressive strength in MPa based on mixture and proportion of input materials and days passed.

  • Bank marketing

    Clustering of clients based on data they provided and information about previous contact. The goal is to help marketing team decide what actions would increase their effectiveness.

  • Walmart sales forecasting

    The goal is to predict sales in a weekly time series data based on store type, binary holiday variable, temperature, fuel price, CPI and unemployment.
